About Me:

Chris Hughes is a Cloud and Cybersecurity SME with nearly 15 years of experience. His previous roles include being active duty USAF, a federal civilian with Naval Information Warfare Systems (NIWC) Atlantic as well as the General Services Administration (GSA) where he served on the Joint Authorization Board (JAB) for FedRAMP. He currently works in the industry, advising and consulting with Federal and DoD leaders on cloud adoption, security and digital transformation. He is also an Adjunct Professor with University of Maryland Global Campus (UMGC) and Capitol Technology University in their M.S. Cybersecurity programs. He is also the co-host of the Resilient Cyber Podcast.
